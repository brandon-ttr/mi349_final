What We Do

We provide a way for teachers to anonymously report their salaries so that other teachers may have an idea about how the reported school district compensates their teachers. We want to enable teachers to have salary transparency even if the internal reporting tools are with the employer.

What's Next

We're currently in development, but we'll provide you with what TeacherLib looks like. We'll open up a testing build soon, once it is ready, we'll make sure we have a way to verify reported salaries!
